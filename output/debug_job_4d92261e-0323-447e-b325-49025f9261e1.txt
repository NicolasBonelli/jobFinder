At Moventi, we are dedicated to guiding organizations through technological transformation with an innovative mindset. We foster a multidisciplinary environment that embraces new technologies and work methodologies. Our central office offers a collaborative workspace equipped with agile tables and recreational areas to create an optimal environment for teamwork and creativity.
As part of our development team, you will contribute to the creation and enhancement of complex web applications supporting various business domains. We focus on integrating microservices architectures and modern front-end frameworks to build scalable, reliable, and efficient solutions that drive client success.

Role and Responsibilities
As a FullStack Developer at Moventi, your core responsibilities will involve designing, developing, and maintaining web applications using Java-based microservices architectures and AngularJS on the frontend. You will work on implementing robust APIs with Spring Boot, managing message flows with Apache Camel, and integrating with SQL databases.
You will engage in the full software development lifecycle, from requirements analysis and design to testing and deployment, ensuring high performance and responsiveness of applications. Collaboration with cross-functional teams is essential, promoting agile methodologies and continuous integration practices.
Key tasks include:
Developing backend services using Java, Spring Boot, and Microservices patterns.
Building and maintaining user-facing components with AngularJS and handling state management using Redux.
Working with Unix-based systems to deploy and operate applications.
Managing message queues and integration flows via Apache Camel.
Ensuring code quality through unit and integration testing.
Adapting to new technologies and tools to enhance development efficiency.
Required Skills and Experience
We seek a highly experienced FullStack Developer with:
At least 5 years of professional experience. The ideal candidate will have deep expertise in

Soft skills such as effective communication, problem-solving, adaptability to dynamic project needs, and a collaborative mindset are crucial. The role demands a proactive approach and accountability in achieving project milestones.
Desirable Skills and Nice-to-Haves
While not mandatory, experience with JPOS drivers for financial message processing will be advantageous. Familiarity with containerization technologies such as Kubernetes and Docker is also a plus, enabling smoother deployment and scalability of services.
Knowledge of ReactJS or other modern front-end frameworks beyond AngularJS will enhance your versatility within our technical environment and support future migration or hybrid frontend strategies.
Benefits and Work Environment
Continuous opportunities for professional growth and ongoing learning within a multidisciplinary environment.
Remote work options, providing flexibility for work-life balance.
Participation in challenging projects that promote skill development, innovation, and career advancement.
Our office is centrally located with excellent facilities including parking and recreational spaces. We cultivate a culture of transparency, collaboration, and innovation, encouraging our teams to take risks and generate impactful ideas in a supportive environment.
